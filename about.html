Our Story

Recipes To Happiness

From creating recipes in Gloria's studio appartment to now selling hundreds of donutes each day, Glazed and Glorious was truly a passion project. After test batching for two years we are now located in the Glebe and we got our recipes just right! Donut stop believing, Glazed and Glorious is here to stay and were bringing back new and exciting flavour each week!

Glazed and Glorious opened it's doors in 2017 after one vegan sweet-tooth was tired of the plain ol classic donut. Don't get us wrong, we love a good old classic here at Glazed and Glorious, but our sweet tooths were always craving more. Every since, we are able to keep our buisness up and running thanks to our loyal costumers!

Meet The Owner

Hi there my name is Gloria Jones and I am the creator of Glazed and Glorious! I am a Plant-Based baker who loves anything sweet! I started baking at the age of 6 but turned vegan at the age of 17. Ever since I felt like my options were low when it came to vegan desrts, especially in Ottawa. Thus came the idea for G+G and the rest is history!
